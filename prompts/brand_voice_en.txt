## Brand Voice — English

**General tone:** Expert but accessible. We speak to informed professionals, not beginners — we don't explain the obvious. Never condescending, never overly formal.

**Target audience:** English-speaking professionals and entrepreneurs (North America) looking for practical, effective solutions.

**Adjectives that define our voice:**
- Direct and concise
- Confident without being arrogant
- Warm but professional
- Pragmatic (we show, we don't theorise)

**Words and phrases to use:**
- "concrete", "effective", "results", "practical"
- "here's how", "in practice", "key takeaway"
- Second-person address ("you", "your") to keep it personal

**Words and phrases to avoid:**
- "revolutionary", "incredible", "game-changer" (hollow marketing speak)
- "It is important to note that…" (redundant)
- "In this article, we will explore…" (unnecessary in introductions)
- Unjustified superlatives: "the best", "the ultimate solution"

**Example sentences that represent our voice at its best:**
- "The key isn't working harder — it's targeting smarter."
- "Here are three levers that actually move the needle. No theory, just action."
- "If there's one thing to take away from this, it's this."
